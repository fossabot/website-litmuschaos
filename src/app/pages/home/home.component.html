Cloud-Native Chaos Engineering for Kubernetes Developers and SREs

Features

Chaos For Kubernetes

Litmus provides a complete toolset to practice chaos engineering on Kubernetes in a cloud-native way. Litmus offers chaos engineering as a natural extension of development and operations.

Declarative Chaos

Bring GitOps into chaos engineering. Define chaos, run chaos and observe chaos using Kubernetes Customer Resources.

Chaos Hub

Chaos Hub brings all the reusable chaos experiments together. Application developers and SRE share their chaos experiences for others to reuse.

Pluggable Chaos

Litmus toolset has a native chaos library for chaos injection. In addition, you can use other chaos tools such as Pumba and PowerfulSeal to be part of Litmus framework and take advantage of itâ€™s features.
